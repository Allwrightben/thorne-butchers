About Us

For over 25 years, we have been at the heart of the Burgess Hill community, proudly running our butcher shop in the Market Place Shopping Centre with passion and integrity. What began as a small local business has grown into a trusted destination for high-quality, ethically sourced meats.

Our mission is simple: to feed our community the best meat possible. We work with carefully selected farms that share our values of sustainability, animal welfare, and exceptional taste. Whether you're planning a Sunday roast or a summer BBQ, you can always count on us to deliver fresh, flavourful cuts that bring people together.
